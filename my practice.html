Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 ½ teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast, sugar, eggs.l
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with the plastic wrap. Leave
    it somwhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size roll it out into a large rectangle
